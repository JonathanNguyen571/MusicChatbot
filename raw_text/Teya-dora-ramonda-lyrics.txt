Teya Dora released her song “Ramonda” on 25th January 2024. The song will later compete in her nation ,Serbia’s , ‘Pesma za Evroviziju 24’ – An annual song contest in Serbia in which the public and a jury vote on which song to send to the Eurovision Song Contest. The song named after the flower, Ramonda Nathaliae, the national flower of Serbia. The flower is capable of surviving extreme conditions, as it can be revived with only a few droplets of water.
SERBIAN
Teya Dora je objavila svoju pesmu “Ramonda” 25. januara 2024. Pesma će se kasnije takmičiti u njenoj naciji, srpskoj, „Pesma za Evroviziju 24“ – godišnje takmičenje za pesmu u Srbiji na kojem javnost i žiri glasaju o tome koju će pesmu poslati. na Pesmu Evrovizije. Pesma koja nosi ime po cvetu Ramonda Nathaliae, narodnom cvetu Srbije.
